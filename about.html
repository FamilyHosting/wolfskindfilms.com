KATHARINA RIVILIS was born 1985

in Saint Petersburg and grew up in Germany.

2003-2007 she studied acting at the UNIVERSITY OF FILM AND TELEVISION „Konrad Wolf“ in Potsdam-Babelsberg, Germany. Since then, she has worked as an actress in various film, television, and theatre productions and also directed her first short films.


In 2012 she began her studies in film directing at the GERMAN FILM AND TELEVISION ACADEMY BERLIN (dffb). During this time, she created the short films “Ariana Forever!” and “DAY X”, which were both shown at numerous international festivals and have received several awards (e.g. Chicago International Children’s Film Festival, Tirana International Film Festival, Festival Européen du Court Métrage de Brest in France, CPH:PIX).


She is currently working on the development of her first feature film “I’LL BE GONE IN JUNE”.
